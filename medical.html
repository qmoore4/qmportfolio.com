Medical

For the medical page, I developed somewhat of a blog post explaining the different types of hemorrhage control. In Fig.1 you'll see that the images under the text, guides themselves into two separate characteristics of hemorrhage. The left image represents external hemorrhage and the right represents internal hemorrhage. Moving onto Fig.2 I give definitions from clicking onto the images added on the previous page. Users can click on the wounds at the bottom of the page, and they'll be guided to other variations of hemorrhage. Due to this project being informational, I added a citation page showing where I received my sources from in Fig.3.
